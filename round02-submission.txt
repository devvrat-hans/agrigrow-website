1. PROJECT TITLE
================================================================================

Agrigrow: AI-Powered Farming Assistant for Indian Farmers

================================================================================
2. PROJECT DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

Agrigrow is an AI-powered platform that empowers Indian farmers through trusted 
knowledge access, peer-to-peer learning, and intelligent crop advisory.

Key Features:
- AI Crop Assistant: Image-based disease diagnosis using Gemini AI
- Community Platform: Peer-to-peer learning through farming communities
- Knowledge Hub: Expert-verified crop guides and pest library
- Multi-language Support: Hindi, Marathi, Telugu, Tamil, and more

Built with Next.js, TypeScript, MongoDB, and Google Gemini AI.
